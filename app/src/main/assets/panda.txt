Habitat
Pandas live in temperate high in the mountains of southwest China.
There are only about 20 isolated habitats where pandas naturally live. 
Red pandas stay in trees while giant pandas live specifically in bamboo forests. 
#####
Diet
Pandas exist almost entirely on bamboo, eating from 26 to 84 pounds per day. 
Pandas are herbivores, so the rest of their diet consist of pumpkin, what, kidney beans. 
They might also occasionally eat eggs, small animals, and carrion(the decaying flesh of dead animals).
#####
Physical Characteristics
The giant panda has a striking coat of black and white, combined with a bulky body and round face. 
The red panda is about the size of a large domestic cat. It has soft thick reddish brown fur above and black fur underneath.
#####
Known For
Pandas have an endearing and captivating appearance that has made them popular animals. That, and their affinity for bamboo
