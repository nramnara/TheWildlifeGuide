Habitat
Different zebras have different habitats.
In general, they usually live in treeless grasslands and savanna woodlands and are absent from deserts, rainforests, and wetlands.
#####
Diet
Zebras are herbivores and feed mostly by grazing on grasses, although they also might browse a bit on the leaves and stems of bushes.
As the dry season arrives and the grasses die back, zebra herds travel to find more food and water holes for drinking.
#####
Physical Characteristics
All zebras are dark-skinned animals.
Hairs that contain melanin appear black, whereas those without melanin appear white creating stripes.
#####
Known For
Zebras are famous for their striped bodies.
#####
IUCN Category
Some Zebras are EN - Endangered. The Plains Zebra are NT - Near Threatened.
