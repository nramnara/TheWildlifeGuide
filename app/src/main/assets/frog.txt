Name
Frog
#####
Species
Amphibians
#####
Physical Characteristics
In general, frogs have protruding eyes, no tail, and strong, webbed hind feet that are adapted for leaping and swimming. They also possess smooth, moist skins.
#####
Habitat
Many are predominantly aquatic, but some live on land, in burrows, or in trees.
#####
Diet
Most frogs eat insects, other small arthropods, or worms, but a number of them also eat other frogs, rodents, and reptiles.
