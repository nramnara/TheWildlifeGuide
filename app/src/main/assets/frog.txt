Habitat
Many are predominantly aquatic, but some live on land, in burrows, or in trees.
#####
Diet
Most frogs eat insects, other small arthropods, or worms.
But a number of them also eat other frogs, rodents, and reptiles.
#####
Physical Characteristics
In general, frogs have protruding eyes, no tail, and strong, webbed hind feet that are adapted for leaping and swimming.
They also possess smooth, moist skins.
#####
Known For
Frogs are known for the large distances they jump and their croaking sound.
