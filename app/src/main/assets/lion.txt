Habitat
Lions live in a variety of habitats but prefer grassland, savanna, dense scrub, and open woodland.
#####
Diet
Lions are carnivores. They usually hunt and eat medium sized to large animals like zebras and antelopes.
#####
Physical Characteristics
The lion is a well-muscled cat with a long body, large head, and short legs. 
Size and appearance vary considerably between the sexes.
#####
Known For
Nicknamed 'King of Beasts', lions are one of the best known wild animals
#####
