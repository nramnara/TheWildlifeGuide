Habitat
All flamingos are found in tropical and subtropical areas.
The flamingo's most characteristic habitats are large alkaline or saline lakes or estuarine lagoons that usually lack vegetation.
Lakes may be far inland or near the sea.
#####
Diet
They eat algae, small seeds, tiny crustaceans (like brine shrimp), fly larvae, and other plants and animals that live in shallow waters.
#####
Physical Characteristics
Flamingos are tall, pink wading birds with thick downturned bills.
They have slender legs, long, graceful necks, large wings, and short tails.
#####
Known For
Flamingos are famous for their bright pink feathers, stilt-like legs, and S-shaped neck.
#####
IUCN Category
The Greater Flamingo is of LC - Least Concern, and the Lesser Flamingo is NT - Near Threatened
