Habitat
Chameleons live in a variety of habitats, from rainforests and lowlands to deserts, semi-deserts, scrub savannas, and even mountains.
Those that hang out in trees are usually green while the ones that live in deserts are most often brown.
#####
Diet
Chameleons generally eat insects such as locusts, mantids, grasshoppers, stick insects, and crickets. 
Some larger chameleons also eat small birds and other lizards. 
A few have been known to eat a bit of plant material.  
#####
Physical Characteristics
Chameleons have long, slim legs, with four feet. Their eyes can move independently of each other and they have prehensile tails that wrap in a coil-like fashion around limbs to maintain balance.  
As is typical of reptiles, they have scaly skin, which can undergo a particular range of color change.
They actually change their colour due to a number of different factors, including their mood (fear, victory, etc), changes in light or temperature, or the humidity of their environment.
#####
Known For
Uber famous for their ability to change color. Many chameleons can assume a green, yellow, cream, or dark brown coloration.
