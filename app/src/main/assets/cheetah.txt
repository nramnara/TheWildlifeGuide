Habitat
Cheetahs inhabit a wide variety of habitats, including the dry, open country and grasslands where they are most often seen, as well as areas of denser vegetation and rocky upland terrain.
#####
Diet
Cheetahs are carnivores that hunt mainly in the early morning and late afternoon.
A cheetah eats a variety of small animals, including game birds, rabbits, small antelopes, young warthogs, and larger antelopes.
#####
Physical Characteristics
Cheetahs are covered almost entirely with small black spots on a background of pale yellow and have a white underbelly.
Their faces are distinguished by prominent black lines that curve from the inner corner of each eye to the outer corners of the mouth, like a well-worn trail of inky tears.
Weight depends on sex, with males being slightly larger than females.
#####
Known For
Cheetahs are the world's fastest land animal, capable of reaching speeds of up to 70 mph.
