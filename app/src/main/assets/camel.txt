Habitat
No matter the type, camels are usually found in the desert, prairie or steppe. 
#####
Diet
Camels are herbivores, eating grass, grains, wheat and oats. Also leaves and branches, bushes, hay, straw, and even thorny plants like cacti.
Their humps store fat that nourishes them and helps them survive long periods of time without eating or drinking.
#####
Physical Characteristics
Generally, camels have long curved necks, humped backs, broad large feet, tufted ails and four teats.
#####
Known For
Camels are famous for their humps and for the fact that they can also go long periods without drinking.
